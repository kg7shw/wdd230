Proposal for a Chamber of Commerce Website

The purpose of this website is to serve as a hub for information and resources related to the local business community. The website will feature news and events, a business directory, member resources, and more.

Scenarios (Target Audience)

  • Local business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Prospective business owners looking to start or relocate a business in the area
  • Community members interested in supporting local businesses

Color Schema

The color scheme for the website will be a combination of dark blue, light blue, yellow gold, and a dark brown. The specific hex codes are:

  • https://coolors.co/036b6e-fbbb2b-002a2b-d5e3e4

  • Primary Color: #036b6e

  • Accent Color 1: #fbbb2b

  • Accent Color 2: #002a2b

  • Accent Color 3: #d5e3e4

Typography

The primary font for the website will be Roboto, with a fallback of sans-serif. This font is clean, easy to read this makes it an ideal choice for this website.
